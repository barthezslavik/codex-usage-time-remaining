## Short Launch Post

I built a tiny Chrome extension for the ChatGPT Codex usage page.

It adds a second progress bar under the weekly quota bar:

- green = quota remaining;
- blue = time remaining in the weekly reset cycle.

Useful for seeing whether you're spending Codex quota faster or slower than the weekly pace.

GitHub: https://github.com/barthezslavik/codex-usage-time-remaining
Support: https://ko-fi.com/barthezslavik
