# Privacy Policy

Codex Usage Time Remaining does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The extension runs only on:

`https://chatgpt.com/codex/cloud/settings/analytics*`

It reads the visible "Weekly usage limit" card in the page DOM to calculate a local time-remaining percentage from the reset date shown by ChatGPT. The calculation happens entirely in the browser.

The extension does not use analytics, tracking, cookies, remote code, external APIs, or third-party services.

This extension is unofficial and is not affiliated with OpenAI, ChatGPT, Codex, Google, Chrome, or Ko-fi.
